XX (byte for X-coordinates)
YY (byte for Y-coordinates)

0x00                            clear buffer
0x01                            update display

5 bytes data
0x02 XX XX YY YY CLOR              drawPixel

6 bytes data
0x03 XX XX YY YY MASK CLOR         drawByte
0x04 XX XX YY YY CHAR CLOR         drawChar

8 bytes
0x05 XX XX YY YY RO RI PT CLOR     drawPie

9 bytes data
0x06 XX XX YY YY XX XX YY YY CLOR     drawLine
0x07 XX XX YY YY XX XX YY YY CLOR     drawRectangle

21 bytes data
0x08 XX XX YY YY CLOR CHAR*16      drawString
0x00 for null
