XX (byte for X-coordinates)
YY (byte for Y-coordinates)

0x00                            clear buffer
0x01                            update display

4 bytes data
0x02 XX YY YY CLOR              drawPixel

5 bytes data
0x03 XX YY YY MASK CLOR         drawByte
0x04 XX YY YY CHAR CLOR         drawChar

6 bytes data
0x05 XX YY YY RO RI PT CLOR     drawPie
0x06 XX XX YY YY YY YY CLOR     drawLine
